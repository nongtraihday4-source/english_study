📌 Bối cảnh

Anna đang ở sân bay và nói chuyện với nhân viên check-in về chuyến bay của cô ấy đến London. Lắng nghe và trả lời các câu hỏi bên phải.

Staff: Good morning! Can I see your passport and boarding pass, please?


Anna: Of course. Here you are. My flight is BA247 to London Heathrow.


Staff: Thank you. How many bags are you checking in today?


Anna: Just one large suitcase. It's about 22 kilograms, I think.


Staff: That's fine. Your seat is 14C, a window seat. Boarding starts at 9:45 at Gate B12.


Anna: Perfect. Is there a lounge I can use? I have a Premier card.


Staff: Yes, the Sky Lounge is on the second floor, near gate B10. Enjoy your flight!
